CoverageCue Privacy

English

CoverageCue stores product names, categories, places or stores, notes, warranty terms, reminder lead times, and purchase dates on your device.

CoverageCue does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not use analytics or third-party tracking SDKs in this release.

Notification permission is optional and is used only for local warranty expiry reminders.

通知权限是可选的，仅用于本地保修到期提醒。
