CoverageCue Support

English

CoverageCue is a local warranty tracker for products you may need to repair, renew, or check before coverage expires.

Add a product, set the purchase date and warranty term, then use reminders and widgets to see what expires next.

For help, contact the developer through the App Store support contact. Read the privacy page.
